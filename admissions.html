Admissions Open for 2025

Join Our Family

Licious High School welcomes all learners who aspire to achieve excellence in academics, discipline, and innovation. Our admission process is transparent, fair, and merit-based.

Requirements

  • Completed application form (available from the school office or website).
  • Recent academic report card or UNEB results.
  • Birth certificate or national ID (for verification).
  • Two passport-size photos.

Admission Process

Applicants can pick admission forms from the school or download them online. Entrance interviews are conducted for new students. Successful applicants will receive official admission letters and orientation details.
